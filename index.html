Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Lalago collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our food delivery services. By using Lalago, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

Lalago collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address for account registration and order processing.
  • Device Information: Location data to show nearby restaurants and deliver your orders accurately.
  • Camera Access: Used for uploading profile pictures and scanning QR codes when applicable.
  • Payment Information: Payment details are securely processed through third-party providers.
How We Use Your Information

Lalago uses your information to:

  • Process and deliver your food orders.
  • Provide customer support and communicate updates about your orders.
  • Improve the functionality of our app and services.
Sharing of Information

Your information may be shared in the following scenarios:

  • With restaurants to fulfill your food orders.
  • With payment providers to securely process transactions.
  • If required by law or government authorities to comply with legal obligations.
Data Security

We implement secure encryption and access control measures to protect your data. While we strive to protect your information, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information through the app's settings.
  • Disable location tracking by adjusting your device settings.
  • Opt out of receiving notifications by updating your app preferences.
